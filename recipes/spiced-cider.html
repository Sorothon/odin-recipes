Spiced Cider

Description

There is nothing better than a warm, sweet, aromatic drink in the colder season.

What better way to taste fall than with Spiced Cider!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon whole cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon whole allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 large orange, quartered with peel
  • 2 quarts apple cider

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place a filter in the basket of an automatic coffer maker. Add brown sugar, cinnamon stick, cloves, allspice, salt, and nutmeg to the basket; top with orange wedges. Pour apple cider into the water reservoir.
  3. Brew and serve hot.
